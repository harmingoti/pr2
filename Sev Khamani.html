Sev Khamani

  • Prep Time: 5 hours, 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 5 hours, 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 250 gm Chana dal
  • 250 gm Sev
  • For Seasoning
  • 2 tsp Oil
  • ½ tsp Mustard seeds
  • 2 tsp Ginger–Chili paste
  • 1 tsp Lemon juice
  • ½ tbsp Coriander seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Pomegranate
  • 1 tbsp Coriander leaves
  • Water according to requirement

Instructions

  1. Soak chana dal for 5-6 hour
  2. Soak chana dal for 5-6 hour and drain water from it.
  3. Grind coarsely.
  4. Now take in a steel tied container and cook for 2-3 whistle in cooker.
  5. Now heat oil in a pan at slow flame.
  6. Add mustard seeds in it.
  7. When it is spluttering, add ginger chili paste, salt, sugar and lemon juice in it.
  8. Stir well for 10-15 minutes and cook till oil is separated.
  9. Now keep in a dish and garnish with Sev, coriander leaves and pomegranate.
