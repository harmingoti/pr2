Basundi Recipe

Baking & Spices

  • 1/2 tsp Cardamom powder / yelakai podi
  • 1 pinch Saffron
  • 3 tbsp Sugar

Nuts & Seeds

  • 1/4 cup Almonds / badam chopped finely
  • 1/4 cup Cashews chopped finely
  • 1/4 cup Pistachios chopped finely

Dairy

  • 1/2 cup Condensed milk
  • 1 liter Milk, Full Fat
