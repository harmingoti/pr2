amla nu athanu

Equipment

  • 1 Kaḍai

Ingredients

  • 10-12 amla
  • 2 tablespoons of rye
  • 1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1 teaspoon raw fennel
  • 1 teaspoon raw fennel
  • ½ teaspoon of rye
  • ¼ teaspoon asafoetida
  • 2 tsp red chilli powder
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. To make amla pickle, first wash and clean the amla, then put a glass of water in a pan on the gas, put a bank in between, put a charni on it, put the cleaned amla in it, cover it and cook it for ten to twelve minutes.
  2. After 10 minutes, when the amla gets cooked, take it out and let it cool down a bit, then remove its slit and keep it aside. Now add rye, fenugreek seeds and fennel seeds to a gas pan and roast it on low heat for two to three to four minutes.
  3. Then take it out in another vessel and grind it in a mixer jar after cooling and keep it aside
  4. Now heat the oil in the same pan, when the oil is hot, add the rai hing and fry it, then add the boiled amla slices and roast it for five to seven minutes.
  5. When the amla is roasted, add red chilli powder, turmeric, salt and ground spices to it, mix it and saute it for another two to four minutes, then switch off the gas and take it out in a jar and enjoy. Amla Pickle
