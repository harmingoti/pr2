Fresh Coconut Halwa

Ingredients

  • 500 gm coconut fresh (grated)
  • 100 gm sugar
  • 150 gm mawa (khoya)
  • 100 gm ghee
  • 2 cup milk
  • 50 gm Almonds (finely chopped)

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee in a pan at medium flame.
  2. Roast coconut in it till it become light pink in color.
  3. Add roasted mawa in it and stir well.
  4. Now add sugar and milk in it and stir continuously
  5. Stir it till ghee separate.
  6. Remove from flame
  7. Garnish with almonds.

notes

  • You can add milk cream instead of milk also.
