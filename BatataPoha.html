Batata Poha

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 200 gm Poha (Rice Flakes)
  • 200 gm Potatoes
  • For seasoning
  • 5-6 Cloves
  • 2 Green Chilies (finely chopped)
  • ½ tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1tsp Sesame Seeds
  • Pinch of Asafetida
  • 2 tsp Turmeric powder
  • ½ tsp Cloves-cinnamon powder
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • 1.5 tbsp Lemon juice
  • 5-6 curry Leaves
  • 2 tbsp Coriander leaves (chopped)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp Peanuts
  • 3 tbsp Oil
  • 1 medium size Onion

Instructions

  1. Boil, peel and cut potatoes in to small pieces.
  2. Wash rice flakes and drain it properly.
  3. Keep a side.
  4. Heat oil in a pan at slow flame.
  5. Add mustard seeds in it.
  6. When it splutters, add sesame seeds, cloves and curry leaves.
  7. Add asafetida, turmeric powder, potatoes, green chili and mix it well. Now add salt, sugar and lemon juice.
  8. Add rice flakes, cloves-cinnamon powder and peanuts in it.
  9. Mix it properly.
  10. Cook 5 minutes at slow flame.
  11. Garnish with coriander leaves and chopped onion.
  12. Kanda Batata poha is ready for serve.

Notes

To make Jain poha, you can skip potatoes and onion.
