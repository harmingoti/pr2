Paan Mukhwas

Ingredients

  • 50-100 betels/paan leaves (washed, wiped, stem cut, chopped, one day or overnight dried by spreading on a cloth inside room). kapuri Paan I used
  • 1 cup water.
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoon Jethimadh/Yashtimadhu/Licorice/Mulethi/Mulhathi powder
  • 2 tablespoons Gulkand
  • 10-15 ml rose water
  • 200-400 gm colorful dry tuty fruity of papaya
  • 200 gm white/colourful coconut fibers/salai topra/reshedar khopra lachchha
  • 50 gm/ 2 tbsp minakshi (any brand) paan chutney (golden sweet syrup with silver varq)
  • 1-1.5 teaspoon amla ber powder/Lovely/Paan masala/Paan bahar/kashmiri sugandh
  • 1 teaspoon Gulab jelly crystal powder
  • 200 gm salai supari/lachchha supari / betel nuts or arecanuts (one colour or multicolored)
  • 200 gm lukhnavi saunf/fennel seeds/variyali (small, sweet naturally)/roasted can be taken whole or crushed
  • 200 gm dhanya daal/roasted coriander seeds used for mukhwas pupose
  • 500 gm dates pieces/ khajur katri (natural colour or multicolored)
  • 50 gm scented dates pieces/ black peppermint flavoured sweet dates
  • 50 gm sanothi/chanothi/hari patti
  • 4-5 pieces menthol/thandak/peppermint crushed as powder
  • 1 teaspoon kattha powder/catechu
  • 20-25 red cherries, deseeded & cut in 8 pieces or whole
  • 1 teaspoon chuna/lime edible
  • 1 teaspoon saunf/variyali powder
  • 10-15 green cardamom with shell/hari elaichi chhilke ke sath
  • as required Sweet, sugar coated colourful jelly
  • as per required Sweet colourful silver, red, green balls
  • as required Sweet coloured sugar coated saunf/variyali/fennel seeds balls
  • as required Sweet supari - crushed finely or powdered - optional
  • as per taste Roasted supari - crushed finely or powdered - as per taste, optional
