gunda nu athanu

Equipment

  • 1 large pan
  • 1 glass jar

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of gunda
  • 500 grams of mango
  • 1 cup Raina Kuria
  • ¼ cup fenugreek paste
  • 2-3 spoons of coriander leaves
  • 1-2 teaspoons of fennel
  • 4-5 teaspoons of turmeric
  • ½ cup red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon asafoetida
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil as required

Instructions

  1. First of all, wash the mango properly and then peel it. Now grate the mango with a grater. Take the grated mango in a vessel and add two or three teaspoons of salt and one teaspoon of turmeric to it.
  2. Now wash the gunda basket with water and dry it in a cloth (make sure that there is no water left) Now break the gunda in the basket by pressing it with your hand or with a stick and with the help of a knife or a stick, remove the seeds/thari from inside. So take out all the gunda seeds and spread them on a cloth (apply some salt on your hands while removing the seeds so that the gunda chikas don't get on your hands)
  3. Heat about 500 to 600 grams of oil in a pan on gas, when the oil becomes 1 gram, turn off the gas and let the oil cool down.
  4. Now in that vessel take rai kuriya, fenugreek kuriya, coriander kuriya, red chilli powder, one spoon turmeric, fennel, two three spoons of salt (salt according to taste) nehing and heat it in a cup of oil that is a little hot from the oil that has been kept for frying. Mix together
  5. Now separate the grated mango from the grated mango that was kept aside and mix it with the prepared spices.
  6. Now take one gunda and fill it with the prepared spices and put the full gunda in a pan or a clean glass jar and after filling the gunda, put the remaining spices on the full gunda and close the pan or jar for 24 hours. Set aside
  7. After twenty-four hours, the oil that was heated and spread on it was added to the gunda to submerge the spices and mixed once with a clean spoon and covered and kept in this way for six to seven days once a day to stir the gunda with a clean spoon after seven days. The pickle is ready
